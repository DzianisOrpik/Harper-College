Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacifc Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails ad is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While aytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trops at sunrise several times a week.
